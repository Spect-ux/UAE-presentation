Early History

Long before oil, the region was home to fishing villages, pearl divers, and traders connected to major global trade routes.

Ancient Settlements

3000 BCE

Archaeological evidence shows early human settlements and trade networks.

Pearl Diving Era

1700s–1900s

Pearling became the backbone of the regional economy.

British Trucial States

1820–1971

The region was known as the Trucial States under British protection.

Formation of the UAE

On December 2, 1971, six emirates united under the leadership of Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan. Ras Al Khaimah joined shortly after.

Union Declaration

1971

The UAE was officially established as a sovereign nation.

Rapid Development

1980s–Present

Oil revenue fueled modernization, infrastructure, and global integration.
