Overview

The United Arab Emirates is a federation of seven emirates located in the Middle East, known for rapid development, safety, and international influence.

Capital

Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi is the political and administrative capital of the UAE.

It holds most of the country’s oil reserves.

Largest City

Dubai

Dubai is the UAE’s largest city and global business hub.

Population

9.5 million

The United Arab Emirates has a population of about 9.5 million people, making it one of the most diverse countries in the world. Emirati citizens are a minority, while most residents are expatriates who come from many different countries for work and better opportunities. People in the UAE come from many ethnic backgrounds, including Arabs, South Asians, Africans, Europeans, and East Asians. The country celebrates this diversity by promoting tolerance, allowing freedom of religion, and hosting cultural events and festivals that bring different communities together and encourage respect and unity.

Language

Over 100+

The UAE is home to a wide variety of languages due to its multicultural population. Arabic is the official language and is widely used in government, education, and cultural traditions. However, English is commonly spoken and serves as a main language for business and daily communication. Many other languages, such as Hindi, Urdu, Malayalam, Tagalog, and Bengali, are also widely spoken, reflecting the diverse communities living in the country and making the UAE a truly multilingual society.
