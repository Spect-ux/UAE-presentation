Overview

The United Arab Emirates is a federation of seven emirates located in the Middle East, known for rapid development, safety, and international influence.

Capital

Abu Dhabi is the political and administrative capital of the UAE.

It holds most of the country’s oil reserves.

Dubai is the UAE’s largest city and global business hub.
